Protecting the Sanctity of J-Space

Wormhole space is not simply another region of New Eden to be farmed, exploited, and discarded at will. It is a unique and sacred ecosystem, home to those who have chosen to embrace its dangers, navigate its shifting pathways, and respect the delicate balance of life within it. Unlike the rigid structures of highsec or the lawless sprawl of nullsec, J-space exists in a state of controlled chaosâ€”where those who live here do so by skill, knowledge, and respect for the unknown.

Unfortunately, not everyone shares this understanding. Some see wormholes as nothing more than temporary ISK-printing machines, dropping in unannounced, extracting resources, and fleeing the moment they sense trouble. Others treat wormhole space as a convenient shortcut, rolling away connections without a second thought to those who depend on them. This disregard for local sovereignty and ecosystem stability is precisely why the Wormhole Police Department (WHPD) exists.
